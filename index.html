Have you ever wonder what houseplant would be a good suit for you? Well here's your chance, using your astrological sign and a selected number, you will be recommended a houseplant!
Golden Pothos (Devil's Ivy)
Scientific: Epipremnum aureum
Golden pothos are very common housplants that are very easy to take of, even for people who are bad with plants.
Zebra Plant
Scientific: Aphelandra squarrosa

Zebra plants get their name from the stark white stripes along their deep green leaves. It survives in room temperature and bright indirect light, making it a perfect houseplant.
Spider Plant
Scientific: Chlorophytum comosum
Spider plants are common houseplants native to South Africa that do not require too much maintenance in order to survive, making it perfect for begineers!
Heart Leaf Philodendron
Scientific: Philodendron hederaceum
This is a popular vine-like houseplant that is known for its heart shaped leaves; it likes to live in indirect light and can even survive off of florescent light!
Heart of Jesus (Fancy-leaf Caladium)
Scientific: Caladium bicolor
This houseplant gets its name for the vibrant color in the center of its leaves that ranges from light pink to a deep hot pink. It can introduced a pop of color for any space in your house.
